McDonald's Christmas Cracker

For the Holiday season, McDonald's installed an interactive digital billboard in London's Piccadilly Circus that displayed a giant Christmas cracker. By visiting a website on their phones, passers-by could pull the cracker with a left or right swipe, trying to win a latte or cheeseburger in the process.
