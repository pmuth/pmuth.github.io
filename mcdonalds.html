McDonald’s Christmas Cracker
In December 2014, McDonald's installed an interactive digital billboard in London's Piccadilly Circus that displayed a giant Christmas cracker. By visiting a website on their phones, passers-by could pull the cracker with a left or right swipe, trying to win a latte or cheeseburger in the process.
As one of two developers on the project, I assisted with developing the mobile front-end, as well as the Node.js server that connected the two phones with the openFrameworks application running on the billboard.
